Prices

2 Person Pod

The Small Pod is 7ft 7in by 8ft 10in and sleeps two adults. The pod is an empty, carpeted space but has a lamp, a heater and a socket. There is a picnic bench outside each pod, and the price includes use of our kitchen facility in the amenity building, which currently has kettles, toasters, sink, fridge, hotplate, but no utensils. You will need to bring all your camping kit except your tent.

4 Person Pod

The large pod is 8ft 7in by 11ft 5in Sleeps 4 comfortably (or 5 cosy!). The pod is an empty, carpeted space but has a lamp, a heater and a socket. There is a picnic bench outside each pod, and the price includes use of our kitchen facility in the amenity building, which currently has kettles, toasters, sink, fridge, hotplate, but no utensils. You will need to bring all your camping kit except your tent.
